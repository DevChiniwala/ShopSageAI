RETURN & REFUND POLICY
----------------------
We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy, here's what you need to know:

- You have 30 days from the date of delivery to initiate a return.
- Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in their original condition with all tags attached.
- Original packaging must be included with the return.
- Free return shipping is provided for all domestic orders.
- Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive the returned item.
- The refund will be credited to the original payment method.
- Exchange requests for a different size or color are also accepted within the 30-day window.
- Sale items are eligible for store credit only — no cash refunds on discounted merchandise.
- Custom or personalized items cannot be returned unless they arrive damaged or defective.
- If you receive a damaged or defective product, please contact us within 48 hours with photos.
